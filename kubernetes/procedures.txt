To bring up:
1. `minikube start [--memory <mb>]` (requires superuser)

Status for running cluster:
* `minikube status`
* `kubectl get pods -A`
* `kubectl cluster-info`

Configure Docker operations to be within cluster:
You can check `docker images`, which will differ when running in/outside of the cluster.
1. `eval $(minikube docker-env)`
